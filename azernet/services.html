SERVICE CENTER

Servix is a service center, created on the basis of existing Azer.Net LLC’s service partners was opened in 2010.

Servix rests upon the idea of professional quality maintenance of computer hardware, on a level of responsibility and client focused approach comparable to that exercised by professional physicians.

Main goal is to satisfy the needs of a growing number of Azerbaijani users of computer hardware in a quality service and improvement of the availability of maintenance services. Servix will be focused at maintenance of finished IT products: PC’s, notebooks,printing devices, UPS’s, digital cameras, and others.

For the first time in the Azerbaijani market, a uniform offer of additional services will be arranged: diagnostics, installation of software, adjustment and repair of hardware. This innovation will provide for transparency of maintenance services and pricing for Servix’s clients.
