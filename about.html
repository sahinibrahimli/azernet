About us

Home / About us

Founded in 2003, Azer.Net LLC now it is a continuously growing and developing business in the IT market of Azerbaijan.

Our mission is to provide consumers all over Azerbaijan with IT products at competitive prices. The company's specialists keep track of major global trends to provide consumers in all regions of Azerbaijan with the widest possible selection of notebooks, netbooks, PCs, components, peripherals and digital devices at the most competitive prices. Azer.Net LLC is a customer-oriented company. The success and prosperity of our partners are top priorities for us. The widest product range and high availability of products, flexible pricing, and an individual approach to clients are our trademark- attractive enough for the most of Azerbaijan computer dealers. Our official business relations with virtually all leading global manufacturers of computer equipment and components are the best proof of our strength, reliability, and predictability. We provide our international partners with wide distribution channels and marketing support all over Azerbaijan territory. Azer.Net LLC has been represented in the Azerbaijani market with the brands of “Nano notebooks & accessories” and “Texno Computers”.

Our company owns 11 shops in Baku and 21 shops in the regions.

In addition to its basic activities, Azer.Net LLC extensively develops the projects in the spheres related to distribution.
